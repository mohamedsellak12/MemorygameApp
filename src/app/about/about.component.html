About the Game

Welcome to the Memory Match Football Edition!

In this game, your goal is to find the two matching cards featuring the same football player. Each card displays an image of a football player, and your task is to remember the positions of the cards and match them correctly before the time runs out.

Game Rules:

  • The game consists of 24 cards, each with a football player's image on one side.
  • You will need to find the two cards that match by flipping them over one at a time.
  • If the two cards match, they remain face up. If they don't, they are flipped back down.
  • You have 3 minutes to match all cards! The game ends either when all cards are matched or when the timer reaches 0.
